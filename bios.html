Biography

where we come together

Evan Barber

Evan is from Salt Lake City, Utah, a location which he describes as being "just as white as you would expect." Despite being asked many times a day, he would like to make clear that he is not a member of the LDS Church and is not a Mormon. He is known for being both attractive and unattractive at the same time, an accolade he has recently come to terms with. He has been to twenty-five of the fifty states and hopes to have visited all of them before he turns fifty years old. He is young, yet feels as old as time. His goal in life is to retire.
