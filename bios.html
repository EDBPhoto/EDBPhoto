where we come together

Evan Barber

Evan is originally from Salt Lake City, Utah. Growing up in such a beautiful state allowed him to develop his keen eye for beauty. Having focused in the past on street and landscape photography, he is currently focused on developing unique portraiture work through the use of vintage manual focus lenses. In his spare time he enjoys playing soccer, reading, hiking or scuba diving.
